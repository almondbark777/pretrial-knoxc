Check-in rules — what counts

Both kinds of contact are required. A client must make an in-person visit and a phone/virtual contact at their level's cadence — a string of phone calls does not cover a missing office visit. The record's Conditions tab tracks each kind separately.

Logging vs scheduling: Log Check-in records a contact that happened (it feeds the compliance math). Schedule Check-in books a future appointment — it shows on the dashboard the day it falls due and turns ✓ once a real check-in is logged that day, but a booking by itself satisfies nothing.

Where the numbers come from

Daily import + your entries, merged
SharePoint data lands once a day (see "Data refreshed" at the bottom of the sidebar). Anything you enter in the app shows everywhere immediately and survives the import.
Same math as the tracker
The console and the classic client tracker compute from one shared engine — the numbers always agree.
"As of" time travel
The date control in the top bar re-runs every page as of any date — for "what did this look like last Monday". CSV exports honor it. Reset with ×.
